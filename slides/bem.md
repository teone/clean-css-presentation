##  BEM
**[Block Element Modifier]**

#### Basic Principles
- Divide you page in **blocks** => _.site-search{}_
- Divide you blocks in **elements** => *.site-search__header*
- Create variants of your block with **modifiers** =>*.site-search__header--fullWitdh*
